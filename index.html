DWI

The point of this engine is to not use textures, sprites,

images at all. Instead, you can draw on the screen using

built-in functions. In this example game, you can press

any key to do a script.

Here are the list of scripts you can do:

draw_one(x,y,r,g,b) Draws one colored pixel.

draw_two(x1,y1,r1,g1,b1,x2,y2,r2,g2,b2) Draws two colored pixels.

draw_three(x1,y1,r1,g1,b1,x2,y2,r2,g2,b2,x3,y3,r3,g3,b3) Draws three colored pixels.

clear() Clears pixels and entire screen.

Runs on GameMaker 8.1
